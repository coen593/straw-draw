Using Straw Draw is easy!

If you want to draw straws with your friends, family or colleagues to settle who needs to do something but are not around each other, Straw Draw offers an easy solution!

By following a couple of easy steps, Straw Draw helps you decide who will need to head out.

  1. Use Create Draw on this website, and fill out the form. If multiple people need to be selected, you can indicate that too.
  2. Once the form is filled in, you will be lead to a page where you can manage your draw, with a URL to share with those you want to participate in the draw. You will receive an email with this information as well.
  3. The participants, following the URL you share with them, will need to enter their details and will receive a confirmation email too.
  4. Once all participants are registered, you can trigger the Draw on your Manage Draw page. Straw Draw will then randomly choose the person/people assigned to your task!
